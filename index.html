About


Jabbar’s General Contracting was developed in 1983 under the name Jabbar’s Carpenter Service in Houston Texas, a sole proprietorship which became a partnership and returned to a sole proprietorship in 1991. The owner, Rahim Jabbar, was trained since high school in building and construction. Trained also in architectural drafting Mr. Jabbar was able to establish a building and construction business that combined technical drawing freestyle art with building experience to create a company distinctly capable of producing a unique design and vision in construction and renovation that separates the company from any other.

Over the past 35 years this establishment has been responsible for building some of the most glamorous private homes in the country, and renovations that prove the concepts of building and construction are limitless. Call Mr. Jabbar with your building and renovation concerns and allow him to take you through journey that will enlighten your building ideas and stimulate your taste for superb living.


Renovations

Every home needs updating after a while. Fortunately, Jabbar’s General Contracting has done hundreds of renovation projects. Everything from bathrooms and kitchens to entire homes, nothing is outside of the scope of what we can do!

Repairs

We can repair any job fast and make sure that your problems don’t become more costly. Jabbar’s General Contracting personally stands by any repair made and guarantees that it will be fixed right!
